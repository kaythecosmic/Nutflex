Stranger Things

ABOUT THE SHOW

Stranger Things is set in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana. The nearby Hawkins National Laboratory ostensibly performs scientific research, but secretly does experiments into the paranormal and supernatural. Inadvertently, they have created a portal to an alternate dimension, "The Upside Down".
